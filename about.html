About the Corps

The National Cadet Corps (NCC) is a tri-services organization, comprising the Army, Navy, and Air Wing, engaged in grooming the youth of the country into disciplined and patriotic citizens.

Army Wing

Focuses on ground combat training, drill, and leadership.

Navy Wing

Provides exposure to naval communications and seamanship.

Air Wing

Teaches aero-modeling, flying, and aviation basics.
